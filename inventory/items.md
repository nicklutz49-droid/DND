# Inventory & Possessions

> What the player carries and owns in goods. The chronicler keeps this current; note
> when items are gained, used up, lost, or given away.

## Carried / Equipped
*(Weapons, armor, clothing, tools on their person.)*
- **The elven blade** — a slender, single-edged, moon-curved sword of elven make, katana-like; carried at the hip. A finesse weapon (Blades / DEX).
- **Hidden boiled-leather jerkin** — discreet light armor worn beneath the finery.
- **The drifter's finery** — charcoal hooded cloak with a circular metal clasp; layered embroidered attire (blue-and-brown geometric); short dark-blue gold-trimmed vest with white lapels; white armband on the left bicep; layered belts (a wide blue sash beneath a pouched brown leather utility belt); tall lace-up boots; dark-brown gloves.

## Pack & Stored
*(Travel gear, supplies, things kept elsewhere.)*
- **Caravan kit** — bedroll, flint & steel, waterskin, a small cook-tin, a coil of cord, a hooded lantern.
- **Scribe's & forger's tools** — pens and spare nibs, several inks, blank papers and a few "borrowed" letterheads, a penknife, sticks of sealing-wax, and a seal-matrix or two not entirely Paige's to own.

## Notable & Personal Items
*(Keepsakes, mysterious finds, quest items, anything with story weight.)*
- **The commonplace-book ("Paige")** — a fat, battered, dog-eared journal: years of scavenged notes, diagrams, half-translated rites, pressed leaves and stone-rubbings. At once Paige's scholarship, their spellcraft, and their magical **focus**. Losing it would hurt.
- **Keepsakes of discarded lives** — a small handful of mismatched tokens kept from names since shed: a signet that isn't theirs, a child's carved animal, a faded ribbon. Each a story Paige doesn't always tell.

## Consumables & Supplies
*(Rations, ammunition, powder, potions, medicine — track rough quantities.)*
- **Trail rations** — a few days' worth.
- **Scholar's sundries** — ink (running low), spare nibs, candle-stubs.
- **A twist of pipe-leaf / tea** — small comforts of the road.
