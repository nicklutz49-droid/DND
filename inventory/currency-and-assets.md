# Currency, Coin & Assets

> The player's wealth and holdings — from pocket coin to, in the late game, businesses,
> property, and nation-scale assets. The chronicler updates after each session.

## Coinage
The frontier runs on a simple metal standard (Empire-minted coin and settler scrip both
circulate; tribes often prefer barter):

- **10 Copper Pennies (cp) = 1 Silver Mark (sm)**
- **10 Silver Marks = 1 Gold Crown (gc)**

*(Barter, favors, and letters of credit matter as much as coin on the frontier.)*
