## The Calendar
*(How time is reckoned on the frontier — developed as it matters.)*
- **Year-reckoning:** Settlers and the Empire date years by **Imperial Reckoning (IR)**,
  counted from the Empire's founding; it is now **IR 1187**. Native peoples keep their own
  counts. *(Finer detail fills in as it comes up in play.)*
- **Seasons:** The standard four. **Winter** slows travel, trade, and campaigning and
  raises danger; **spring** reopens them; harvest, festivals, and tribal gatherings recur.
- **Months / weeks:** Imperial months; high summer falls in the month of **Highsun**.
  *(The fuller calendar fills in when it's needed.)*

### Campaign log
*(Dated session events recorded here by the chronicler, newest at the bottom.)*
- **IR 1187, 6th of Highsun — [on-screen]** — **Session 1.** Paige, an elven drifter and
  self-taught scholar, steps off Dalia Renn's trade caravan into the river-town of
  **Selvecross**, looking for work and a fresh start. Buys supplies, takes cheap lodging near the
  Drowned Crow, and looks for work — meeting imperial surveyor **Coll Verrane**, who needs a scholar
  to read elder ruin-marks. Paige takes the job and joins his small upriver expedition.
- **IR 1187, ~7th–8th of Highsun — [on-screen]** — **The Drowned Stair.** ~1.5 days up the Wend to a
  half-submerged elder ruin. Paige reads the marks but deliberately delivers a **false reading** so the
  worked magic seal will not loosen — and discovers the ruin is a **prison/threshold holding an ancient
  sentient entity**, which makes direct contact. Paige feigns curiosity, learns what it is, then pulls
  back and warns Verrane the place is dangerous ("something tried to possess me"). Hands over a partial
  reading, demands and gets **triple pay**, and parts from the expedition.
- **IR 1187, 8th of Highsun — [on-screen]** — **The Pact.** That night Paige secretly resolves to return
  alone. Re-supplies, runs back up the Wend (practicing magic the whole way), and at the Drowned Stair
  bargains with the imprisoned entity — striking a **pact**: Paige keeps control and gains power; the
  entity gains, over time, freedom and Paige's fair counsel. Paige's magic is dramatically enhanced —
  the **Unseen Hand rises to Apprentice**, and a pact-granted **Earthen Affinity** (earth-sense, shaping,
  sinking, tree-felling, stone-hurling) blooms atop it. *(Hidden costs not yet apparent to Paige.)*
- **IR 1187, ~9th–11th of Highsun — [on-screen]** — Paige drills the new powers in the woods — combat,
  defense, utility — twice pushing near **Spent.** Returns to Selvecross and takes a **bounty** off the
  Drowned Crow work-board (posted by **Borric Tann**, backed by **Hald Brennar**) to stop the river-raiders
  preying on barges.
- **IR 1187, ~12th–14th of Highsun — [on-screen]** — **The raider hunt at Kettle Narrows.** Paige goes on
  foot up a side-creek off the Wend, uses earth-sense and tracking to find the camp, frees the captive
  bargeman **Garran**, and isolates and interrogates a lone raider — uncovering that the "raiders" are
  **settlers in disguise, paid by the Empire** to stage raids, **mark the kills as Wendfolk work**, and
  frighten Selvecross into begging for a garrison. Paige sets a **kill-zone** at the creek pinch and breaks
  the band: stones at range, felled trees, survivors sunk waist-deep in hardened earth. **Kills two**
  (the sentry, who cuts Paige's forearm; one man under a felled oak), **two escape into the dark**, and
  **captures the boss, Calder** (ex-imperial officer), alive. Loots silver, an imperial blade with the
  stamp filed off, command-tent papers, a barge-schedule ledger proving a **town informer leaked the
  wharfmaster's records**, and a **locked strongbox.** Loads four prisoners, Garran, and all the evidence
  into the raiders' boat.
- **IR 1187, 14th of Highsun (late afternoon) — [on-screen]** — Paige runs the captured boat down the
  Wend and Selve into the **Selvecross docks**. A crowd gathers; someone runs for Brennar's man. Under
  her cloak, Paige opens the strongbox (key from Calder's neck): a **wage-purse of marked imperial crowns**,
  **three unsigned officer's letters** (target list "marked as Wendfolk work"; payment due "when the
  Council sends to the river-fort for soldiers"; "report only to **the advisor** — the survey office is not
  to be troubled with this," sealed with a **closed-eye-over-vertical-line sigil**), and an informer's slip
  ending "**— to R., at the Crow, same as before.**" **Session ends on the cliffhanger:** the box open,
  Brennar's man approaching, Paige weighing what to hide and what to reveal.
