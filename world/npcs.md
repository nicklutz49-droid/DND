# Known NPCs

> **Known** people: who they are, how they regard the player, and their current status. The
> chronicler adds newly-met NPCs and updates dispositions, deaths, and open threads. Unmet
> figures and the **secret agendas** of known ones live in `dm-secrets/hidden-npcs.md` —
> only what the player knows belongs here. Promote a revealed secret here with its in-game date.

## Disposition Scale
`Hostile · Wary · Neutral · Friendly · Loyal · Devoted` — always note *why* it stands where it does.

### Dalia Renn
- **Who they are:** A settler **caravan-master** — weathered, shrewd, and plain-spoken; runs goods up the Selve trade-road into the Selvemark. Well-known and well-owed the length of the river.
- **Disposition toward player:** **Friendly** — took a liking to Paige on the long road in, and vouched for them on arrival at Selvecross.
- **Status:** Alive & well — in Selvecross now, turning her cargo around before the next run.
- **What the player knows about them:** She knows the river-road's people, debts, and gossip; knows who's hiring in town and who's best avoided.
- **Open threads / debts / promises:** Vouched for Paige (a foothold in a strange town). May have work or a lead — and the favor runs both ways.

### Coll Verrane
- **Who they are:** A soft-spoken **imperial surveyor** with a charter and good coin, based at the **Ferryman's Rest** in Selvecross; the visible face of the Empire here. Hires scholars to read the elder ruin-marks because he cannot read them himself.
- **Disposition toward player:** **Wary / Neutral** — businesslike; Paige spooked him at the Drowned Stair, but he paid up and they parted on civil terms.
- **Status:** Alive & well — around Selvecross. **Does not know Paige went back to the ruin alone.**
- **What the player knows about them:** Hired Paige to read marks at a drowned elder ruin up the Wend; **paid triple** the agreed fee after Paige reported "something tried to possess me — this place is dangerous" and handed over partial sections. Paige privately **suspects his survey is a cover for something else** — he is far more interested in *threshold/ward* marks than in fur or farmland, and was vaguer than he should be about why.
- **Open threads / debts / promises:** Paige fed him a deliberately **false reading** (skipped load-bearing marks, mispronounced joins) so the seal would not loosen — he believes he has a true partial. The deception, and Paige's suspicion of him, are both live. He flinches at the word "advisor."

### Borric Tann
- **Who they are:** The Drowned Crow's quick, ferret-faced **work-broker and fixer**; posts the work-board and deals odd jobs for a cut.
- **Disposition toward player:** **Friendly** — Paige is now a proven earner who delivered on a hard job.
- **Status:** Alive & well — Selvecross, at the Drowned Crow.
- **What the player knows about them:** Posted the **river-raider bounty** (backed/blessed by town boss **Hald Brennar**, on behalf of the river-merchants). Knows who needs what done quietly.
- **Open threads / debts / promises:** **Owes Paige the raider bounty** — not yet collected.

### Mother Sable
- **Who they are:** Keeper of the **Drowned Crow** inn and taproom; missing two fingers, forgets nothing, knows every face on the river and trades gossip as freely as beer.
- **Disposition toward player:** **Neutral / Friendly** — a familiar lodger now; well-disposed.
- **Status:** Alive & well — Selvecross, at the Drowned Crow.
- **What the player knows about them:** Runs the common room where Paige first found lodging and work. Her taproom is **where the informer "R." drinks** — a lead Paige holds but Sable doesn't yet know she's part of.
- **Open threads / debts / promises:** A potential source on "R." and on river gossip, if Paige works her carefully without tipping her hand.

### Hald Brennar
- **Who they are:** The **town boss** of Selvecross — owns the ferry, the biggest timber-crews, and the hard men who keep order. Genial, immovable, watching everything that lands.
- **Disposition toward player:** **Neutral** — about to have his **first real meeting** with Paige; his man is walking up the dock to summon her.
- **Status:** Alive & well — Selvecross.
- **What the player knows about them:** He **backed/blessed the raider bounty.** He is the man Paige must now answer to about the prisoners and the evidence — and the man who will most want to know who's behind the raids.
- **Open threads / debts / promises:** The imminent dock meeting. How much Paige reveals to him — about the frame-up, the imperial coin, the informer "R.," "the advisor" — shapes everything next.

### Calder
- **Who they are:** The captured **boss of the river-raider band** — an **ex-imperial officer** running a crew of settlers-in-disguise paid by the Empire to stage raids and frame the Wendfolk.
- **Disposition toward player:** **Hostile** — beaten, bound, and humiliated by Paige.
- **Status:** **Alive but wounded** — cracked ribs, split lip; **Paige's prisoner** at the Selvecross docks. Bogged in mud and hauled aboard his own boat after the fight.
- **What the player knows about them:** Ran the false-flag operation; carried fresh imperial-mint coin (a *wage*, with a quartermaster's tally-string), an imperial blade with the stamp filed off, and a locked strongbox of damning orders. He **reported "only to the advisor"**; the orders bore a closed-eye-over-vertical-line sigil and kept the survey office deliberately in the dark.
- **Open threads / debts / promises:** **His fate is undecided** — hand him to Brennar, hold him for leverage, interrogate him further, or something else. He is a living witness to the Empire's dirty work — and to Paige's outlawed magic.

### Garran
- **Who they are:** A **bargeman** the raiders had taken captive at their camp; freed by Paige during the hunt.
- **Disposition toward player:** **Friendly / grateful** — Paige cut him loose and brought him home.
- **Status:** Alive & well — returned to Selvecross by boat with Paige.
- **What the player knows about them:** A working riverman who can **corroborate Paige's account** of the raid, the camp, and the rescue — a friendly witness at the docks.
- **Open threads / debts / promises:** Owes Paige his life and freedom; a witness who saw Paige use earth-magic in the fight (a double edge — corroboration *and* a mouth that knows).
