# Character Sheet — Paige

> Elven drifter, scholar, and self-taught dabbler in the unseen arts.
> Created **IR 1187, 6th of Highsun**, at the start of Session 1.

---

## Identity
- **Name:** **Paige** — current alias. Born **Tharthradion Hérunadar Shaalvaas** *(family · given · birth-city)*.
- **Origin & background:** An **elf** of the distant city of **Shaalvaas**, raised largely by an uncle who put a sword in their hand "so you can always walk away from trouble." A long, restless, eccentric life since — a dozen trades and a dozen discarded names across decades. "Paige" is only the latest: a scholar's pun, and a nod to the battered notebook they are never without.
- **Why the frontier:** **A fresh start, drifting.** Rode into the Selvemark on a settler trade caravan with no grand plan — just work, coin, and curiosity about where this life leads.
- **Appearance & bearing:** Slender, athletic, and graceful, moving with a fluid, unhurried ease. Shoulder-length tousled chestnut hair; an angular, expressive face; pale eyes under striking brows; a relaxed half-smile that suggests they know something you don't. Dressed richly and oddly for a drifter — a charcoal hooded cloak pinned with a circular clasp over layered, embroidered attire in blue-and-brown geometric patterns: a short dark-blue vest with gold-trimmed white lapels, a white armband on the left bicep, layered belts (a wide blue sash beneath a pouched brown leather utility belt), tall lace-up boots, dark gloves. Beneath the finery, a **discreet boiled-leather jerkin**.
- **Age:** ~160 — old enough for a dozen lives, young enough to keep starting over.

### Magic Known
**The Unseen Hand** *(Apprentice — self-taught, then quickened by a pact; tradition uncertain).* The kinetic knack Paige pieced together from scavenged notes, now sharply deepened by a bargain struck at the Drowned Stair (see *Traits › The Pact*). The general telekinesis rose a tier; the **earth-and-stone aspect bloomed far further**, into a distinct **Earthen Affinity** that performs at roughly Journeyman level for soil and stone alone.
- **The unseen hand** *(Apprentice)* — telekinesis: lift, shove, pull, or fling objects a short way; trip a latch; guide their own blade for a beat; turn a blow. **Fine control remains the weak end** — delicate, precise work still slips.
- **Earthen Affinity** *(pact-granted; ~Journeyman, earth & stone only)* —
  - **Earth-sense** — feel through soil and stone: footfalls, bodies, the shape of the ground; locate people and movement at a distance through the earth.
  - **Shaping** — harden or soften ground at will; raise, sink, and mold it; **hardened earth** to bind, gag, or trap (sink a body waist-deep and clamp it fast).
  - **Force of the deep roots** — lever standing trees over by their roots; hurl stones with real, bruising, bone-breaking force.
- **Costs & tells:** still **draining** — Paige has twice pushed near **Spent**. Still **conspicuous and loud** — stone grinds, ground heaves, trees groan and fall; impossible to hide from witnesses. Still **unlicensed**: unsanctioned magic is **outlawed and hunted** by the Empire, and Paige has now worked it openly in front of several. And it carries the pact's hidden hook.

## Edges
- **A face that won't stick** — people struggle to place or precisely remember Paige; descriptions never quite match. A serial reinventor's blessing — and a mercy when an old name comes hunting.
- **The long view** — decades and a dozen trades leave Paige with surprising scraps of practical know-how about an odd range of things.

## Flaw
- **A past name comes due** — somewhere out there, one of Paige's discarded lives left a debt and someone who wants paying. Sooner or later, a creditor, a mark, or an old enemy will recognize a face that "won't stick."

## Traits Gained in Play
- **The Pact** *(IR 1187, 8th of Highsun — the Drowned Stair).* A bargain struck, eyes open, with the **ancient sentient entity** sealed beneath the Drowned Stair. Its terms, as Paige set them: **Paige keeps control**; the entity lends power; in exchange Paige grants it freedom over time (agreeing to work, slowly, at loosening its seal) and will weigh its counsel under fair consideration. It is the wellspring of the Earthen Affinity above — and an open account whose **full price is not yet known.** Paige struck it deliberately, expecting to outplay it; the entity agreed without haggling, which is itself a thing to remember.

## Ties to the World
- **Connection:** **Dalia Renn**, the settler caravan-master who brought Paige up-river and vouched for them at Selvecross — a first foothold, and a favor that runs both ways.
- **Personal goal:** *To find, for once, a life — or a name — worth keeping.*

- **Standing reputation:** Becoming **locally notable** in Selvecross — "the stranger who brought the raiders in."
