# Hidden Lore — DM ONLY

> Secret history and deep truths of the frontier — the land's real past, the origin of
> magic, what the old ruins are, what the tribes remember that settlers don't. Develop to
> support the metaplot and reward investigation. Promote to `world/world-bible.md` when
> the player earns each truth.

## Seeds
- **The land's old power.** Something predates all three powers here — the thing the
  Empire's design is truly after (tie to `empire-metaplot.md`). What is it, where, and
  what does it cost to use or hold?
- **The truth about magic.** Why is it rare and feared on the frontier specifically?
  What do the tribes know about it that settlers don't?
- **The ruins.** Who built them, why are they abandoned, what still sleeps there?
- **A buried wrong.** Some past atrocity or pact whose consequences are still unspooling.
